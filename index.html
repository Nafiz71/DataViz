Exploring Tech Job Market in Australia

The tech industry in Australia is rapidly growing. This visualization shows key insights into the tech job market, salary trends, and top companies. Understand where opportunities lie in this fast-evolving field.

Salary Distribution Across Australian States

Tech jobs in metropolitan areas offer higher salaries, especially Sydney and Melbourne. Regional areas show lower salaries but emerging opportunities exist.

Estimated Salary Distribution by Tech Job Titles

Roles like Software Engineers and Data Scientists offer the highest salaries, while Entry-Level roles start lower but offer room for growth.

Job Title Frequencies in the Tech Market

Software Engineers, Data Analysts, and Consultants dominate the Australian tech market.

So What are the Top Tech Companies to Get Hired By?

Deloitte, EY, and CSIRO offer competitive salaries and exciting opportunities. These companies are great places to start your tech career.

So What Are The Highest Paying Tech Related Roles?

Software Engineers, Data Scientists, and Engineers top the salary charts, with base salaries over $120K. Data Analysts earn below $100K.
