Exploring Tech Job Market in Australia

The tech industry in Australia is rapidly growing.

This visualization shows key insights into the tech job market,

salary trends, and top companies.

Understand where opportunities lie in this fast-evolving field.

Salary Distribution Across Australian States

Tech job salaries across Australia show stark variations.

Metropolitan areas, especially Sydney and Melbourne, exhibit higher salaries.

Salaries in these cities exceed $120,000 AUD on average.

Regional areas show lower figures, but growing opportunities exist.

Estimated Salary Distribution by Tech Job Titles

Roles like Software Engineers and Data Scientists lead the market,

with base salaries averaging over $130,000 AUD in large companies.

Entry-level positions start around $80,000 AUD,

but provide growth opportunities in the tech industry.

Job Title Frequencies in the Tech Market

The treemap below shows the frequency of tech roles in the market.

Software Engineers, Data Analysts, and Consultants lead the market.

Consultants are increasingly important as companies focus on tech optimization.

Over 30% of tech roles posted in 2023 are for these positions.

So What are the Top Tech Companies to Get Hired By?

Several companies in Australia offer competitive salaries and diverse opportunities.

Tech roles are abundant in multinational firms, startups, and growing sectors.

Companies like Deloitte, EY, and CSIRO lead the market.

Starting salaries often exceed $100,000 AUD in these organizations.

So What Are The Highest Paying Tech Related Roles?

Software Engineers, Data Scientists, and Engineers top the salary chart.

Base salaries for these roles exceed $120,000 AUD.

Data Analysts and Business Development Managers earn below $100,000 AUD.
