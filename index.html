Exploring the Australian Job Market

This collection of visualizations offers insights into the job market in Australia, with a focus on job titles, salary estimates, and company sizes across various states.

This choropleth map shows the distribution of average base salaries across Australian states, helping to visualize salary trends across the country.

The box plot represents the distribution of estimated base salaries for various job titles. The range and outliers highlight salary variations across different job positions.

The bar chart showcases the top 10 job titles in terms of average salary. It helps to compare how salaries vary between different job roles in the Australian job market.

Visualizations by Nafiz71. The data used in this visualization is sourced from various public datasets related to job salaries and company sizes across Australia.
