Exploring Tech Job Market in Australia

This set of visualizations shows the Tech job market in Australia. Whether you're looking for a job in technology or an analyst position, this visualization provides key insights into the Australian tech job market. It highlights dominant roles, salary trends, and top companies, helping you make informed career decisions and understand where opportunities lie in this fast-evolving field.

So What are the Top Tech Companies to Get Hired By?

Several companies in Australia offer competitive salaries and diverse opportunities.

Tech roles are abundant in multinational firms, startups, and growing sectors.

Companies like Deloitte, EY, and CSIRO, known for their reputation and offering competitive salaries

are great places to start your career, particularly with larger firms typically offering starting salaries above $100K in tech roles.

So What Are The Highest Paying Tech Related Roles?

Software Engineers, Data Scientists, and Engineers top the salary chart with an average base salary of over $120K, showcasing their high demand in the market. In contrast, Data Analysts and related roles tend to earn below $100K, emphasizing the salary gap between technical and analytical positions in the tech field.
