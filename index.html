Exploring Data Science Job Market in Australia

This collection of visualizations offers a comprehensive analysis of the Data Science job market in Australia. We focus on key factors such as job titles, estimated base salaries, and the sizes of companies hiring across various states. By examining salary distributions and geographic patterns, these visualizations provide valuable insights into the evolving demand for Data Science roles and the economic landscape influencing them. Whether you're a job seeker looking to align your career trajectory or an employer seeking to understand industry trends, these insights can help you navigate Australia's competitive Data Science market.

This choropleth map shows the distribution of average base salaries across Australian states, helping to visualize salary trends across the country.

The box plot represents the distribution of estimated base salaries for various job titles. The range and outliers highlight salary variations across different job positions.
